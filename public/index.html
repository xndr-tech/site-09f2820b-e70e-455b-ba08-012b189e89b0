About StaticSiteTest

StaticSiteTest was born from the need for reliable and efficient static site deployment. We provide a dedicated platform for developers and teams to confidently push changes, knowing that comprehensive end-to-end tests will validate their work.

Our mission is to eliminate deployment anxieties by offering a streamlined process that integrates seamlessly with your existing CI/CD pipelines. From small personal projects to large-scale enterprise applications, we ensure your static sites are always performant, secure, and error-free.
